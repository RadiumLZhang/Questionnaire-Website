Perceived Harmfulness of Anti-Asian Hate Speech

Goal of Research: This research aims to study the harmful narratives of anti-Asian hate speech amidst the COVID-19 crisis. Understanding these narratives is crucial for comprehending the messages conveyed, identifying the targeted entities, and assessing the impacts on these communities. The findings will be instrumental in designing effective intervention strategies.
